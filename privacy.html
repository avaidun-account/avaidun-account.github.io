PlateSwap Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

PlateSwap is committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Meal photos you upload for scanning (processed by OpenAI, not stored permanently)
  • Your selected goal (stored locally on your device)
  • Scan history (stored locally on your device)

How We Use Your Information

We do not sell your data. Photos are sent to OpenAI's API for analysis only and are not retained beyond the immediate request.

Camera Access

Camera access is used only when you choose to scan a meal or menu.
