Caire AI

A home health triage reference tool. Describe a symptom in plain text and optionally attach photos or lab result PDFs — the app simultaneously queries Claude, GPT-4o, and Gemini in parallel, then compares their responses to produce a structured urgency assessment, consensus summary, and a printable report to bring to a doctor. Privacy by design: no database, nothing stored, every request discarded after processing.

  • Gate screen with plain-language disclaimer — clear that this is a reference tool, not a diagnosis
  • Accepts up to 4 files — images base64-encoded, PDFs text-extracted server-side
  • Parallel API calls to Claude, GPT-4o, and Gemini — returns urgency level (1–4), plain-language summary, and doctor talking points per model
  • Consensus view — where models agree, where they differ, and what to watch for
  • Printable report for doctor appointments

Mobile app (iOS/Android) that helps users make smarter food choices based on their personal goal — Lose weight, Maintain weight, or Gain weight. Scan a meal photo, search by name, or scan a barcode; GPT-4o Vision analyzes the food and either suggests a goal-aligned swap or confirms a great choice with positive reinforcement.
